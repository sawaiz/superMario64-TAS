Mupen 

Mupen64 is a Nintendo 64 emulator. The rerecording version is currently maintained by the Mupen64 Organization under the name mupen64-rr-lua. 
Mupen was disallowed for submissions in 2017, but reallowed in 2022 . The AVI fixes have been merged into upstream as of version 1.1.1 . Older versions should not be used for submissions. 
Links 
Website: https://mupen64.com 
Repository: https://github.com/mupen64/mupen64-rr-lua 
Releases: https://github.com/mupen64/mupen64-rr-lua/releases 
Downloads 
The official Mupen64 repack - a bundle of Mupen64 and TAS-ready plugins - is available at https://mupen64.com/. 
Downloads of Mupen64 binaries are available under the Mupen64 GitHub repository . 
Versions of Mupen64 older than 1.0.5 (including 0.5!) are vulnerable to a savestate-based code execution exploit ("Queuecrush"), and should therefore not be used.
